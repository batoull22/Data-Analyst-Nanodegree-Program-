Introduction¶

In this project, you'll go through the analysis of TMDb movie data using NumPy, pandas, and matplotlib. This dataset contains information about 10,000 movies collected from the movie database (TMDb) and also contains many features which are id, budget, revenue, original_title, cast, etc.

Questions:¶

  1. Which year has the highest release of movies?
  2. What is the highest revenue movie?
  3. What is the least revenue movie?
  4. What is the average revenue of the movies?
  5. What is the average runtime of all movies?
  6. What is the highest budget movie?
  7. What is the least budget movie?
  8. What is the average Budget of the movies?

Conclusions¶

Finally, we can summarize our findings and answer questions.

  • The highest release of movies was 2014, the number of the movies nearly 700.

  • Movies that achieved the highest revenues were in the year 2009 and the lowest revenues in the year 2003 and 1995.The average revenue of the movies equal to 39823319.7.

  • Average runtime of the movies equals 102.07 minutes.

  • The Warrior's Way has the highest budget and 'Fear Clinic', 'Lost & Found', 'Angus', 'Thongs and Perfect Snogging' and 'Love, Wedding, Marriage' has The lowest budget.
